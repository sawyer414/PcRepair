About Me

Hi, I'm Sawyer Teed, a student at Romulus High School and a computer programming student at Finger Lakes Technical and Career Center. I've developed a strong interest in computers through both my studies and hands-on experience building and repairing PCs.

Over time, I've worked on assembling custom computers, diagnosing hardware issues, and bringing systems back to life. What started as a personal interest has grown into a skill set I'm proud of—and now I'm looking to turn that into a small business.

My goal is to provide reliable, affordable PC repair and support services to people in my community. Whether it's fixing hardware problems, upgrading components, or helping build a custom system, I focus on doing the job right and making sure everything runs smoothly.
